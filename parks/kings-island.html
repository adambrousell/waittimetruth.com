KINGS ISLAND WAIT TIME ACCURACY

Cedar Fair · Mason, OH · Opened 1972 · Ranked #15 of 20 parks · 143 guest reports

Crowdsourced guest reports show that posted wait times at Kings Island run an average of 7 minutes higher than the actual wait experience. The overall posted-to-actual ratio is 1.18×, classified as Moderately Off in our Park Accuracy Index.

Kings Island offers Fast Lane Plus ($60–$150+/day) as its paid skip-the-line option. Guests considering this purchase may want to compare the posted wait against the actual accuracy data below.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How accurate are Kings Island wait times?

Kings Island shows a posted-to-actual ratio of 1.18× — close to accurate. Posted times typically run about 18% higher than actual waits. Mystic Timbers and The Beast tend to show slightly wider gaps, while Banshee and Flight of Fear are among the more accurate rides.

Is Fast Lane Plus worth it at Kings Island?

Fast Lane Plus at Kings Island ranges from $60 to $150+ depending on the date. With posted times averaging only 18% higher than actual waits, the gap between posted and actual is relatively small — meaning the wait you see on the sign is a fairly reliable indicator of what you'll experience.

How does Kings Island compare to Cedar Point?

Kings Island (1.18×) shows slightly less accurate posted times than its sister park Cedar Point (1.12×). Both Cedar Fair parks are significantly more accurate than Disney (1.22×–1.48×) and Universal (1.34×–1.79×) parks. The Cedar Fair chain generally shows strong wait time accuracy across its properties.
