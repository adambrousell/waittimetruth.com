WaitTimeTruth.com is a consumer advocacy platform that tracks theme park wait time inflation by comparing posted queue times against actual guest-reported waits across 20+ major U.S. parks.

Standard Description (1 paragraph)

WaitTimeTruth.com is a consumer advocacy project by Amusement Standards LLC that tracks whether theme parks inflate posted wait times. Using data from the Queue-Times.com API (which pulls directly from parks' official systems) and crowdsourced guest reports, WaitTimeTruth calculates an Inflation Factor for every major ride at 20+ U.S. parks including Disney World, Disneyland, Universal Orlando, Epic Universe, Six Flags, Cedar Point, Dollywood, and more. The average park overstates posted wait times by approximately 38%. WaitTimeTruth is a RatingValue.org project and the companion site to AmusementRating.org, a crowdsourced guest-side operations audit platform for the attractions industry.

About Amusement Standards LLC

Amusement Standards LLC is a risk intelligence company based in Asbury Park, New Jersey, founded by Adam Brousell. The company builds data analytics platforms for the attractions industry, including the AS 360 risk intelligence system for insurers, AmusementRating.org for crowdsourced guest audits, and WaitTimeTruth.com for wait time inflation tracking. All consumer-facing platforms operate under the RatingValue.org umbrella brand.
